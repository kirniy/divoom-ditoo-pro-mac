# Pixel Animation Direction

The Ditoo is 16x16, so the right design language is not tiny poster art. It is motion-first abstraction with deliberate silhouettes and restrained palettes.

## Working styles

- `orbit`: luminous orbital paths with a moving highlight core
- `plasma`: dense electric gradients with high contrast edges
- `ripple`: calmer field animation with watery pulse structure

## Why these work on 16x16

- They survive extreme downsampling.
- They read as motion before they read as detail.
- They can be looped cleanly.
- They do not depend on legible typography or tiny icons.

## Use cases

- ambient desk animation
- codex / claude live status
- notification pulse
- “busy / available / cooling down” device states
